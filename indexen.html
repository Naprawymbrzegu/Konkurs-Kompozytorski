About the Competition

In 2025, Czechowice-Dziedzice will host an extraordinary artistic event: the composition competition "On the Right Bank of the Vistula...", aimed at young composers from Poland and abroad.

The goal is not only to promote contemporary accordion music but also to create a platform for a new generation of composers to showcase their ideas, visions, and individual musical language.

Czechowice-Dziedzice is not coincidentally the place for this initiative. It is here that Andrzej Krzanowski ‚Äî a distinguished composer and accordionist ‚Äî lived and worked. The competition marks the 35th anniversary of his untimely death.

The results will be announced on October 28, 2025, during the final concert in Czechowice-Dziedzice ‚Äî an evening that will celebrate new music and conclude the first edition of the competition.
