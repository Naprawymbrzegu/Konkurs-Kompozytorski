Regulations

1. Organizers
The competition is organized by: Andrzej Krzanowski Foundation, Small Music Academy in Czechowice-Dziedzice, Municipal Cultural Center in Czechowice-Dziedzice.

2. Purpose of the Competition
The competition aims to promote the work of young composers and enrich the contemporary accordion repertoire.

3. Participants
The competition is open to composers up to 35 years of age (born after October 1, 1990), regardless of nationality.

4. Repertoire
Works for accordion solo, accordion with electronics, accordion with other instruments, and accordion ensembles are accepted.

5. Submission Deadline
October 1, 2025. Compositions received after this date will not be considered.

6. Awards
Prizes totaling 10,000 PLN will be awarded, including the Grand Prize, 1st, 2nd, 3rd place, and special mentions.

7. Jury
The jury consists of six members who are experts in contemporary music and accordion repertoire.

8. Other Provisions
Participants agree to the publication of their compositions and recordings as part of the contest's promotion.
