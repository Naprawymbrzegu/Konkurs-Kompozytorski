Regulations

1. Organizers The competition is organized by:
  • The Andrzej Krzanowski Foundation
  • The Little Academy of Music in Czechowice-Dziedzice
  • The Municipal Cultural Centre in Czechowice-Dziedzice
2. Purpose of the Competition The aim of the competition is to promote the work of young composers and to enrich the contemporary repertoire for accordion.
3. Participants The competition is open to composers of any nationality who are up to 35 years old (born after October 1, 1990).
4. Terms of Participation Each participant may submit one original composition.
  • The composition must not have been previously published, publicly performed, or awarded.
  • It should last 6 to 8 minutes.
  • It must be written for solo accordion, optionally including electronics (e.g., stereo tape, live electronics).
The organizer does not provide support for the electronic part ‚Äî it should be self-contained or performable by the accordionist.
