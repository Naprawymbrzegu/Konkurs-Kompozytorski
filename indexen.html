About the Competition

In 2025, Czechowice-Dziedzice becomes the venue for an exceptional artistic event: the composition competition "On the Right Bank of the Vistula...", aimed at young composers from Poland and abroad.

The competition's goal is not only to promote contemporary accordion music, but primarily to create a space where the new generation of creators can present their explorations, visions, and individual musical language.

It is not by chance that Czechowice-Dziedzice hosts this initiative. This is where Andrzej Krzanowski lived and created ‚Äì an outstanding composer and accordionist, a charismatic figure who deeply inscribed himself in the history of Polish contemporary music. The competition takes place on the 35th anniversary of his premature death.

The results will be announced on October 28, 2025, during the final concert in Czechowice-Dziedzice ‚Äì an evening that will become a celebration of new music and a symbolic culmination of the competition's first edition.

Regulations

1. Organizers The competition is organized by:
  • Andrzej Krzanowski Foundation
  • Small Academy of Music in Czechowice-Dziedzice
  • Municipal House of Culture in Czechowice-Dziedzice
2. Competition objective The competition aims to promote the work of young composers and enrich the contemporary accordion repertoire.
3. Participants The competition is aimed at composers up to 35 years of age (born after October 1, 1990), regardless of nationality.
4. Participation rules Each participant may submit one original composition.
The work:
  • must be previously unpublished, not performed publicly, and not awarded
  • should last from 6 to 8 minutes
  • must be written for solo accordion, with optional use of electronics (e.g., stereo tape, live electronics)
The organizer does not provide realization of the electronic layer during the concert ‚Äì it should be self-sufficient or possible to perform by the accordionist.
5. Submission deadline and method Submission deadline: until October 1, 2025 (the date of receiving the message is decisive).
Results announcement will take place on October 28, 2025, during the "Triptych" concert in Czechowice-Dziedzice.

Applications should be sent exclusively electronically to:

  • declaration: "I declare that I am the author of the submitted work, which has not been previously performed, published, or awarded. I accept the competition regulations."
